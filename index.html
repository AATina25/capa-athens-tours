Half-Day Tours

Athens Highlights Mythology Tour (4 hours)

Discover the myths behind Athens’ greatest landmarks! Visit the Acropolis, Temple of Olympian Zeus, Ancient Agora, and more, while learning about Greek gods and heroes.

Beat the Crowds Afternoon Acropolis Tour (2 hours)

Explore the Acropolis in the late afternoon, when crowds thin out and the light is perfect. Learn about its history with a top-rated licensed guide.

The Delicious Athens Food Tour (3.5 hours)

Discover Greek flavors and their history with a stroll through Athens’ neighborhoods. Enjoy tastings of olive oils, cheeses, souvlaki, and sweet treats like baklava and loukoumades.

Unexpected Athens Orientation Tour (4 hours)

Experience real Athens by exploring five contrasting neighborhoods, from ancient to modern. See how different cities coexist and intertwine over time.

The Olympic Games Workout (2 hours)

Capture the true Olympic spirit with a fun outdoor experience. Practice ancient skills and race at the world’s first modern Olympic Stadium.

Cape Sounio & Athens Riviera Sunset Tour (5 hours)

Visit the Athens Riviera, the Niarchos Cultural Center, and the Temple at Sounio to witness an unforgettable sunset.

Day Trips

The Ultimate Delphi Day Trip (12 hours)

Explore the ancient site of Delphi, nearby villages, and enjoy authentic Greek cuisine in a local tavern.

Extraordinary Mycenae & Nafplio Day Trip (11 hours)

Visit Mycenae’s Lion Gate and the charming town of Nafplio. Enjoy artisan demonstrations and a delightful local lunch.

A Dazzling Day Trip to Hydra (11 hours)

Explore the picturesque car-free island of Hydra, enjoy horse rides, water taxis, and a visit to a local pastry shop.

Enchanting Aegina Island (11 hours)

Discover Aegina’s Temple of Aphaia, medieval capital, and local farm where you’ll make rice pudding and taste traditional cheeses.
