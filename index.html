Half-Day Tours

Athens Highlights Mythology Tour (4 hours)

Discover the myths behind the greatest landmarks in Athens! Enjoy a unique 4-hour mythological tour that will help you understand how Athens became one of the greatest cities in the world and a beacon of civilization. Our expert guide will let you know all about Greek gods and heroes in an entertaining way and will help you discover what really hides behind the universal myths! At the same time, you’ll get to visit the most impressive landmarks and sites in Athens: The majestic Acropolis (the Parthenon, the Theater of Dionysus, the Temple of Athena Nike, The Erechtheion, the Sanctuary of Asclepius), the Temple of Olympian Zeus, the Ancient Agora, and the temple of Hephaestus! A fun and lively experience that will give you real insight.

Beat the Crowds Afternoon Acropolis Tour (2 hours)

The afternoon hours are the best time of the day to visit the greatest landmark in Athens. As the crowds have thinned and the summer heat has eased, you’ll visit the world-famous Acropolis in ideal conditions. Learn about Greek gods and heroes from your top-rated licensed guide and see the universal monuments on the Acropolis hill up close in this 2-hour afternoon tour.
